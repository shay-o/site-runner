Start at [https://tool.redoio.info/](https://tool.redoio.info/bias_analysis)
Click "Load" button
In the section "Find Cases" in the text box under "Tell us what you're looking for from the variables above. Be as specific as possible:", enter "Armed robberies in Alameda county"
Click the "Run" button below the text box.
Under "Select Ethnic Groups", choose "White" and "Hispanic" in the multi-select tool
Click "Calculate Bias Metrics"
Wait for the explanation to be generated. Text will appear on the page saying "### Bias Analysis Report" 
Click the "Statistical Analysis (.docx)" button.
Confirm a file named "bias_detection_report.docx" is downloaded.
